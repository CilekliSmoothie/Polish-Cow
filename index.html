Polish Cow

On October 19, 2015, the Polish artist Cypis Solo released the track “Gdzie jest biały węgorz”. The title of the song is translated as: “Where is delirium tremens?”

The lyrical hero of this song talks about drug withdrawal. He sings about how bad he is and how he cannot get rid of addiction. And all this accompanied by a cheerful melody. The track begins with the words of Tylko Jedno W Głowie Mam (“I only have one thing on my mind”). The clip was posted on YouTube. Over 5 years, the video has gained 18 million views. However, the song received viral popularity only in 2020. It all started in mid-July. For some reason, youtubers started using the track for their humorous videos. People would take a melody and put it on a video of someone dancing. On July 13, 2020, YouTuber Dejman made a video of an ordinary cow dancing to Polish music. A similar GIF without music was taken as a basis.
